Who We Are

Pawfect Match is a compassionate animal shelter based in Carrollton, GA. Our organization is dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and finding loving homes for animals in need. We believe every animal deserves a second chance and a safe place to call home.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a world where every pet finds a loving family. We are committed to promoting adoption, educating the community about animal welfare, and providing resources to ensure a better future for pets and their owners. Join us in making a difference, one paw at a time.
